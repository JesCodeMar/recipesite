Milkshake Recipe

A perfect milkshake is thick enough for a spoon to stand in but still drinkable. Any mix-ins should still be in little pieces, and there should be a really pure ice cream flavor thatâ€™s not diluted with milk.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pint ice cream (about 2 cups)
  • 2/3 cup milk, preferably whole

Instructions

  1. Let the ice cream soften.
  2. Chill your glasses and chop any mix-ins.
  3. Layer the ingredients in the blender.
  4. Blend the milkshake.
  5. Pulse in the mix-ins.
  6. Serve the milkshake.
